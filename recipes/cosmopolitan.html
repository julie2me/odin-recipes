Cosmopolitan

History of the Cosmopolitan

Bartender Neal Murray says he created the cosmopolitan in 1975 at the Cork & Cleaver steak house in Minneapolis. According to Murray, he added a splash of cranberry juice to a Kamikaze and the first taster declared, "How cosmopolitan."[8] This event supposedly led to the naming of the new beverage.

Ingredients

  • 4 cl Vodka Citron
  • 1.5 cl Cointreau
  • 1.5 cl Fresh lime juice
  • 3 cl Cranberry juice
Garnish
orange peel or lime wedge
Drinkware
Martini cocktail glass

Mixology

  1. Shake all ingredients in cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  2. Strain into a large martini glass.
